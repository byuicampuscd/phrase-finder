Learn

  • Learn, Do, Become Wheel 
  • Announcements - Check you instructors announcements for the week. 
  • Questions and Conversations - For your questions about the course or your assignments.
  • Supplemental Reading - Prophetic teachings relevant to this week's scripture block.
  • Digging Deeper - Watch at least six videos that provide insight into the doctrines and principles of this week's scripture block.

Do

  • Directed Study - Select an option from the Directed Study guide to help you focus on a significant principle or theme from this week's scripture block.
  • Doctrinal Discussion Board (small group discussion) -Share your insights from the scripture block with your classmates and instructor, and learn what insights  your classmates are having.
  • Course Project 10 Minute Talk - Write a talk on a significant principle or theme from the Book of Mormon as though you were invited to speak in a Church meeting. Your talk should be 4-5 pages double spaced which is about 10 minutes in length. 

Become

  • Personal Study Report - Report on your completion of this week's activities.
  • Mid Course Evaluation
