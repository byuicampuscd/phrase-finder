You will need to download this printable chart. On this chart you will be prompted to come up with your own questions--based on another class that you are taking or textbook that you are reading--according to the different levels of Bloom's Taxonomy.

For example, if you are taking a class on Natural Disasters a first level question would be "What is an earthquake?" These questions are supposed to be written based on a course you are taking not you talking about the course to another person, so asking "Have you ever been in an earthquake?" will not get you points. Stop thinking about date mode now and apply Bloom's Taxonomy to a real life class you are taking or a work related issue could be acceptable as well.
