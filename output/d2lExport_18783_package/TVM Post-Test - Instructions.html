Complete the TMV Post-Test in I-Learn. Here are a few guidelines or tips to avoid issues with the TVM module:

  1. You will only be allowed to access the post test once.
  2. Use Excel formulas and not the Present Value (PV) tables to calculate the answers. If you use the PV tables, the system will score correct answers as zero due to rounding differences.
  3. Keep it simple. Input the calculated amounts without commas, dollar, dashes or negative signs. For example, $50,250 would be input as 50250.
  4. For questions calculating a rate, calculate the answer to four decimal places. If the calculation generates an answer of .0435, input the number as 4.35 without the % sign.
  5. If less than .5 round the calculation down and do not round up.
  6. See Notes from Instructor for additional information and explanations on the Excel present value formulas and factors.
  7. Listen and interact with the entire TVM presentation. You cannot skip ahead since your access will be timed within I-Learn. Once you have completed the presentation, the system will automatically generate a green check mark on the left hand side. You don't have to "mark complete" the presentation. You can take the post-test after you see the green check. If you are still having trouble accessing the post-test, exit and re-enter I-Learn or Brightspace.
