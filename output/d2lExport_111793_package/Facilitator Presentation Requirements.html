Facilitator Presentation Requirements

Helps

  • Active Stakeholder Participation: An Agile Best Practice
  • Sprint Review Meeting Cartoon (Flash format)
  • Effective Agile Meetings

Description

It is recommended (but not required) that each student facilitate at least 1 interactive stakeholder meeting during the semester. Keep the following in mind:

  • Identify the goals of the session (what does each stakeholder group need to understand more clearly)
  • Encourage interactivity
  • Clarify and capture key points (write them down, use feedback to refine existing work, add them to a backlog, etc)

Deliverables

  1. Personal presentation rubric or how you want the audience to evaluate you (5 characteristics of good presenters)
    • Don't just focus on what you are good at, select 2 strengths and 3 areas of growth
    • Discuss the rubric with the Presentation Practice Center
    • Fine-tune the rubric to incorporate their feedback
