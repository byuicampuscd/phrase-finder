Learn

  • Learn, Do, Become Wheel - Click on the sections of the Wheel at the top of this page to view supporting scriptures and quotes. They will help you think about the Learn, Do, Become cycle of change you are implementing each week in your efforts to become a better disciple of Christ. 
  • Announcements - Be sure to check out the things your instructor posts on the Announcements page: 
  • Questions and Conversations is the place to post any questions you have so your classmates and your instructor can help you find answers.
  • Digging Deeper - Watch at least six digging deeper videos to gain further insight into the doctrines and principles discussed in the scripture block. 

Do

  • Directed Study - Select one of the options provided to help you better understand a significant principle or theme within the scripture block this week. Use your personal study to help you complete your chosen assignment. Later, you can share some of these insights with your classmates in the Doctrinal Discussion Board. (30 min in addition to your Personal Study time)
  • Personal Study (Alma 30-35)
    • Option 1: Read the entire scripture block - This option is ideal for those who want to familiarize themselves with the basic story line and teachings of the Book of Mormon.
    • Option 2: Spend at least 30 minutes each day studying within the scripture block - This option is better suited for individuals who already know the basic story line within the scripture block and would like to spend more time studying its doctrines and principles. (30 minutes five days per week)
  • Doctrinal Discussion (small group discussion) - Discuss with some of your classmates the insights, doctrines, and principles you are learning from this week's scripture block and your Directed Study assignment. Try to share something that could be uplifting or encouraging for your classmates to better understand. 

Become

  • Personal Study Report - Report on your completion of this week's activities.
