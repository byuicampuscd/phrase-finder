Instructions

One of the groups you are likely to participate in at some time in your life (if you have not already) is the Ward Council.

You may have a preconception that Ward Councils always work well together because they are “about the Lord’s work.” However, these groups do not always remain productive. In fact, they regularly go through the process of establishing productive group dynamics. Even though they are imperfect, there is strength in understanding how the Lord intends groups to work for Him. As you watch these training videos from the Church, note on a piece of paper what you notice about the advice you receive. Is it similar to what you have learned so far in this lesson? What are the similarities? What is unique? How can you apply the same principles in groups for school and work?
