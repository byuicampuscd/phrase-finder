Lesson 04: Prepare

Reading

Read L04 Readings

As you read through this information and watch the video, consider the main ideas and prepare for the activities in Teach One Another and Ponder and Prove. You will want to review those activities prior to completing the reading and watching the video.

Video

Prior to watching this video, review the Teach One Another and Ponder and Prove activities to see how the information in this video fits in to the lesson as a whole.

Watch “A More Perfect Union” (112 min). There are captions in the video if needed.

Additional Resources

The remainder of your prepare time should be spent pursuing ideas or questions that were brought to your attention while studying the reading materials. It is an opportunity for you to add to your own learning and then serve your fellow students by adding this additional insight to the Teach One Another activities.

You may want to discuss the lesson materials with parents, or ask someone you know and respect to act as a mentor and suggest other materials for you to study. You may also want to correspond with other students.

Use the FDAMF 101 Library Research Guide resource linked to from the Lesson 01 > Course and University Resources Module.
