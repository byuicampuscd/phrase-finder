  1. Dream Makers and Dream Breakers]
    • This PowerPoint and video invites you to examine people who you might classify as either dream makers or dream breakers. As you view the PowerPoint and the video, try to identify people in your life who are key dream makers and those individuals who could be dream breakers. As you progress on your entrepreneur journey, you will encounter people along the way who will be dream makers. these will be your mentors and people who will help you succeed. Life also has its dream breakers, people who don't have your best interest at heart, or who may simply discourage your efforts.
