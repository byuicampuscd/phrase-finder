Lesson Introduction: Perennialism and Progressivism

"Most of what I really need to know about how to live, and what to do, and how to be, I learned in kindergarten. Wisdom was not at the top of the graduate school mountain, but there in the sandbox at nursery school. These are the things I learned. Share everything. Play fair. Don't hit people. Put things back where you found them....Learn some and think some and draw some and paint and sing and dance and play and work every day".

-Robert Fulghum All i really need to know, I learned in Kindergarten

Objectives/Outcomes

  1. Know the differing views Perennialism and Progressivism has of: the learner, curriculum, instructional methods, classroom management, assessment and the role of the teacher.
  2. Know major contributions of leading advocated for Perennialism and Progressivism.
