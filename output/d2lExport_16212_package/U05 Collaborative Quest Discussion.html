Unit 5: Collaborative Quest Discussion

Body image is a central issue of adolescence and even adulthood for many women. Eating disorders and other extreme measures are often the result of an obsession with obtaining the ideal body or physical perfection. View the following clips and share with your group the pressures you have observed in our physically obsessed culture. What suggestions or counter measures would you give to help your daughter develop a healthy body image?
