Preparation: Promoting Healthy Weights

Estimated time to complete: 2.5 hours.

Learning Objectives and Questions Guide

  • You will be able to describe the potential advantages and concerns with weight loss prescriptions/supplements.
  • You will be able to describe recommendations for healthy weight loss.
  • You will be able to describe eating behaviors and patterns to promote healthy weights.
  • You will be able to identify the role of fitness and exercise on overall health and weight management by describing appropriate recommendations.

The preparation guide lists questions you should be able to answer and key terms for the objectives. It will be very useful as a reference in the timed lesson quiz. (Click to download guide)
