Group Set Creation For Instructors


If you would like to create Adobe Connect Groups for your class, please follow these instructions:
  1. In the top left corner, click on "Course Menu".
  2. Go to the "Course Plan".
  3. Click on the "Group Setup" tab located next to "Course Outline", "Grading Categories", etc...
  4. In the bottom left corner, click on "Add" and select "Group Set".
  5. Include the word "Adobe" in the "Group Prefix" field, otherwise groups won't work. You may add the word "Group" as a separate word after "Adobe", then group numbers will automatically be assigned at the top of the columns when groups are generated. You do not need to include your own group numbers.
  6. Select a "Group Assignment" as desired.
  7. Please try to limit the number of total groups to 10, unless your course requires more groups.
  • Lastly, click on "Generate Groups".

Note: Students will not see this page until the groups are created.
