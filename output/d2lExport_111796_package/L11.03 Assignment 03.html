Assignment 03: Torque

Objective

Explain, in terms of torque, how to open a door.

Assignment

Read section 12.5 in your textbook.

Exercise

Everything you need to know about torque you can find out trying to open a door.

  1. Push on the door near the hinges. Notice that this isn’t as effective as pushing on the door near the outer edge of the door.
  2. Grab the doorknob with both hands and lean backwards. Notice that the door doesn’t open farther this way.
  3. Open the door the way you usually do. Notice the 90° between a line from the rotation axis and the direction of the force.

You can think of torque as the tendency for something to rotate. The product of two quantities defines torque: (1) the lever arm, and (2) the force. Always look for 90 degrees. You’ll learn more about this in Lesson 13.
