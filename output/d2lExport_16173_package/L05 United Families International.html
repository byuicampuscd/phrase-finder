United Families International

The videos below are recordings of a field trip to the Utah Capitol building in Salt Lake City by the FAML 460 students in the Fall of 2012. In this field trip, which campus students have the opportunity to participate in every semester, they meet individuals with vast knowledge and experience in family policy and legislation, the use of social science research, and other pertinent topics. In this section, the videos are all about individuals involved with United Families International.

Campus students tend to rate this field trip as one of the most meaningful ones of their entire BYU-Idaho experience. While the recordings certainly do not do justice to the experience and excitement of meeting some of these people face-to-face, we hope you will still feel inspired, increasing your passion and ability to advocate for the eternal unit of the family.

Please see the links to the bios of a couple of the speakers below so that you know a little about each person you will watch.
