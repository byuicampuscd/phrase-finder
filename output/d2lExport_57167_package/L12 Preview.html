Learn

  • Learn, Do, Become Wheel (1 min)
  • Announcements - Check your instructors announcements for the week.
  • Questions and Conversations - For your questions about the course or your assignments.
  • Supplemental Reading - Prophetic teachings relevant to this week's scripture block.
  • Digging Deeper - Watch at least six videos that provide insight into the doctrines and principles of this week's scripture block.

Do

  • Directed Study - Select an option from the Directed Study Guide to help you focus on a significant principle or theme within this week's scripture block.
  • Doctrinal Discussion Board (small group discussion) - Share your insights from the scripture block with your classmates and your instructor, and learn from the insights they have to share with you.
  • Course Project Ten Lessons from the Book of Mormon - For this project you will create a PowerPoint or similar presentation to present ten of the most important lessons you have learned while studying the Book of Mormon this semester.

Become

  • Personal Study Report - Report on your completion of this week's activities.
