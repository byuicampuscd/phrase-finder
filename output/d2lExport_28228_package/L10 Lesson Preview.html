Learn

  • Learn, Do, Become Wheel (1 min)
  • Announcements - Check your instructors announcements for the week.
  • Questions and Conversations - For your questions regarding the course and your assignments.
  • Supplemental Reading - Prophetic insights relevant to this week's scripture block.
  • Digging Deeper - Watch at least six videos that provide insight into the doctrines and principles within this week's scripture block. (20 min)

Do

  • Directed Study - Select an option from the Directed Study guide to help you focus on a significant principle or theme from this week's scripture block.
  • Personal Study (Enos - Mosiah 3) - Study within the scripture block for at least 30 minutes each day.
  • Doctrinal Discussion Board (small group discussion) -Share your insights from the scripture block with your classmates and instructor, and learn what insights  your classmates are having.

Become

  • Personal Study Report - Report on your completion of this week's learning activities.
