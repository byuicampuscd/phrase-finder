L07: Companionship Study

Outcomes

  • Gain an appreciation for the thoughts and feelings that new members experienced as they learned of the Plan of Salvation.
  • Teach your companion about the Plan of Salvation through sharing your personal experiences, testimonies, and a meaningful scripture(s).
  • Identify and incorporate ways in which you could improve your own teaching based upon the models of teaching excellence provided by returned missionaries.
  • Give feedback to your companion regarding his or her teaching, including strengths and areas of improvement.

Study

  • PMG, pp. 47-59, The Plan of Salvation
  • Missionary Handbook, pp. 22-28 "Missionary Conduct"

(Studying these materials will enable you to better prepare for missionary service by helping you complete part of the Pre-MTC checklist)
