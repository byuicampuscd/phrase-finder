Lesson Introduction: Becoming a Teacher

"As teachers you stand upon the highest peak in education, for what teaching can compare in priceless value and in far-reaching effect with that which deals with man as he was in the eternity of yesterday, as he is in the mortality of today and as he will be in the forever of tomorrow. Not only time, but eternity is your field."

-Pres. J. Reuben Clark, Jr.

Objectives/Outcomes

  1. Become familiar with expectations of the course, instructor, and classmates.
  2. Gain a better understanding of the teaching profession.
  3. Analyze their reasons for choosing education as their major including reviewing the rewards and challenges to teaching.
