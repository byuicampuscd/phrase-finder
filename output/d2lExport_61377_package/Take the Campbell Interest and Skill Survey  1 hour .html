Activity Directions

For this activity you will take the Campbell Interest and Skill Survey. This survey compares your self-reported interests and skills with people in a variety of occupations.

Step 01

Before you take the survey, select the link below to watch a video tutorial on how to take the survey. The video will open in a new browser window.

[

Video: Campbell Interest and Skill Survey Part 1

]

http://[courses.byui.edu] /PSYCH_112/Campbell/Part1/Part1.html

(If you would like to read a transcript of this video, download and view the attached file.)

Step 02

Contact the Academic Discovery Center.

Academic Discovery Center

(208) 496-9800

academicdiscoverycenter@byui.edu

Manwaring Center Room 129

Tell them you would like to take the “Campbell Interest and Skill Survey.” The Academic Discovery Center will send you an email with your log-in information. This survey will cost $12 to take. Your student account will be charged.

Step 03

Click on the link in your email to access the Campbell Interest and Skill Survey online. Then use the log-in information to log into the site. Follow the directions and begin. The survey will take about 45 minutes to complete. When you have completed the survey, save and print your results.

Step 04

Select the “Mark this activity complete” button to let the instructor know you have completed this learning activity.
