Lesson 09: Prepare

Reading

Read L10 Readings

As you study the prepare materials, consider the main ideas and prepare for the activities in Teach One Another and Ponder and Prove. You may want to review those activities before you begin studying the prepare materials.

Video

Watch "America in the 20th Century: The Civil Rights Movement" (84 min) or read the transcript (provided alongside the video).

Prior to watching this video, review the Teach One Another and Ponder and Prove activities to see how the video fits into the lesson as a whole.

Gateway Seminar Reminder

Remember to complete the Gateway Foundations Seminar Assignment if you have not yet done so. This activity is found in the Gateway Seminars module.

Additional Resources

The remainder of your prepare time should be spent pursuing ideas or questions that were brought to your attention while studying the reading materials. It is an opportunity for you to add to your own learning and then serve your fellow students by adding this additional insight to the Teach One Another activities.

You may want to discuss the lesson materials with parents, or ask someone you know and respect to act as a mentor and suggest other materials for you to study. You may also want to correspond with other students.

Use the FDAMF 101 Library Research Guide resource linked to from the Introduction > Course and University Resources module.
