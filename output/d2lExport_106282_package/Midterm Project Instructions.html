Midterm Project Instructions

For the midterm, you will be asked to develop a “research presentation” from one of the topics found in the list below.   You will select a topic and do a “deep dive” into that material. You could create a narrated presentation (PowerPoint or Prezi) or create an essay document. Whatever format you choose to submit it in, make sure you check with your instructor to see if they can open the file you turn in. Your work needs to be completed by you and demonstrate your effort in digging deeply into the materials. Feel free to review the materials in the previous lessons as you make your selection. You are required to go beyond the materials presented in class.

List of Topics to Choose From

  • Classical Approaches
  • Human Relations and Human Resources Approaches
  • Systems and Diversity in Organizations
  • Organizational Culture
  • Learning, Innovation and Creativity in Organizations

Required Elements

  • Include at least three outside resources (this means using the electronic resources at BYU-Idaho Library or other credible sources).
  • Must go beyond the material presented in the course lesson.
  • Present the material in an interesting way. Include visual or audio elements or other media to make your presentation engaging.
  • Should take about ten to fifteen minutes for someone to read/watch/listen to it (time a friend).
    • Narrated Presentation (PowerPoint or Prezi) should be about 10–15 slides, with speaker notes and narration
    • An essay should be 5–10 pages, including appropriate images and media. Use good judgement. If you fill up 4 pages with images, it will not take 10 minutes to read through your document.

Narrated PowerPoint presentations can be very large which can make it difficult for the student to upload and the instructor to download. For those who are submitting a presentation, it is highly suggested that you can save the presentation as a video. In PowerPoint, save it as an MP4 using the Save As function. Or you can use Screencast-O-Matic. This is a free screen recorder for instant screen capture and sharing found at http://screencast-o-matic.com/home.

After you have prepared your video, upload it to YouTube or use Screencast-O-Matic’s Save feature. When finished, just submit the link to prevent technical problems and ensure your assignment can be seen.

Help

If you have questions about the project, use the Questions and Conversations forum to ask. You may also wish to read through the FAQ - Midterm Project page.

If you need help writing your paper, please take advantage of the BYU-Idaho Writing Center. Through the Writing Center website, you may schedule time to meet with a tutor in an online chat room. A tutor will offer suggestions and explanations of key writing principles as they relate to your work. If you think you may need help with writing, don't wait until the last minute to schedule an appointment.
