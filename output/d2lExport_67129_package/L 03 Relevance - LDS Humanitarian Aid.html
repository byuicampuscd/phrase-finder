Instructions

Mormons are known for helping. You have likely spent many days, weeks and even years serving others.

But even we, as a Church organization, have to coordinate our work and train for specific activities. Every calling in your ward or branch requires coordinating, training and motivating volunteers. Rely on the experience you have in your church work and let others know you are ready to engage in the community to work with volunteers.

Preparation must occur prior to the storm. How will you prepare to work with volunteers in your profession? Please watch the related videos below to learn more about the humanitarian aid that the Church provides.
