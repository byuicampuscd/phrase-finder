Development Team Setup Notes - MATH 221

General Notes (General things needed)

Make sure the course Grading Scale matches the scale in the Syllabus; if the Syllabus does not specify a Grading Scale, use the BYUI (Standard) scale

The first 3 exams are worth 100 pts and the 4th exam is worth 125 pts

Adobe Connect Online Meeting Tools

Virtual Office only, no student groups needed.

Restrict Access Dates

Give access to the learning activities for the next week's lesson on Thursday at 12 PM (Mountain Time) (noon) before the week the lesson is taught.

Assignment Due Dates

For 1st lesson within each week, make Group Preparation quiz due Tuesdays at 11:55 PM (Mountain Time)

For 2nd lesson within each week, make Group Preparation quiz due Thursdays at 11:55 PM (Mountain Time)

Homework assignments for both lessons are due on Saturdays at 11:55 PM (Mountain Time)

Allow Late Submission on all assignments for Week 1, Week 2 and Quizzes (Homework).

After Week 2, all assignments have a grace period of 1 day except Exams.

Exams

  • If the exam is the first lesson of the week, it will open Monday at 11:55 PM (Mountain Time) and close Wednesday at 11:55 PM (Mountain Time)
  • If the exam is the second lesson of the week, it will open Thursday at 11:55 PM (Mountain Time) and close Saturday at 11:55 PM (Mountain Time)
