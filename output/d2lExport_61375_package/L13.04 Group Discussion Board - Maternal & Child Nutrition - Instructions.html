Topic: Feeding Children

Question to answer: What is the best way to feed children?

[

Much of what we choose to eat as adults depends on what we learn to eat as children. Caregivers are responsible for deciding what foods are offered and when and where they should be eaten. Read the Ellyn Satter Division of Responsibility in Feeding (click here). Watch the "Feeding Baby Lauren" video (click here). Read The Family: A Proclamation to the World (click here).

]

Ideas for discussion:

  • Depth: List some creative, healthy snacks that you might give to your children. Include some ideas for managing childhood food aversion.
  • Breadth: What is the stewardship of the parent and what is the agency of the child in the feeding process? How can you make these two work together?
  • Meaning: At a recent visit to the family pediatrician, Susie was told her son is at risk for childhood obesity. Susie is concerned and would like to do make changes; however, she is concerned about making her son feel bad about his weight. How would you suggest addressing this issue?
