2. The Smith Family

[Watch The Smith Family video (transcript). This video contains the case study for chapter 6 & 7. After watching the video write a 200-300 word reflective response using the questions below and submit your paper.
]
  1. What feelings did you have as you watched the video? What did you notice about your reaction? Are you aware of any biases? Did your feelings suggest any biases you might have if you were assigned to work with a family such as the Smith Family? If this is not a family that would be difficult for you to work with, think of one that would be difficult and describe why briefly.
  2. What does this video suggest to you about being a social worker and working with diverse populations?
  3. What would you need to do if you were to work with persons whose values or behaviors differ significantly from what you believe and how you conduct your life?
