Preparation: Vitamins

Estimated time to complete: 2.5 hours.

Learning Objectives and Questions Guide

  • You will be able to describe basic characteristics of vitamins.
  • You will be able to identify the key functions, food sources, deficiencies, and excessive intake concerns for several of the water-soluble vitamins.
  • You will be able to identify the key functions, food sources, deficiencies, and excessive intake concerns for the fat-soluble vitamins.

The preparation guide lists questions you should be able to answer and key terms for the objectives. It will be very useful as a reference in the timed lesson quiz. (Click to download guide)
