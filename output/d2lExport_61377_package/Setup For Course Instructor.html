Setup For Course Instructor

General Setup for Instructor

Complete the Instructor Pre-Semester Checklist.

Ensure that your Instructor Profile is updated with your Office Hours and other information. To do this, follow the steps below:

  1. Click on your name in the upper-right corner of I-Learn and then choose "Profile" from the drop-down menu.
  2. Click the Change Picture button and upload your BYU-I professional photo. It must be a small version of it to fit.
  3. Include other information that you would like your students to see. Under "Homepage," enter your Adobe Connect link (https://byui.adobeconnect.com/... (the beginning of your email address). Under the Contact Information section, be sure to include your phone number and email address. Students can click your image anywhere in the course and it will bring up your profile page. Likewise, you can do the same for students.
  4. Open your course from the Select a Course dropdown menu in the top black minibar and click in Content. Find the Welcome module and click the drop down menu next to the Welcome page and select "Edit HTML." Update this page with your contact information along with other information you'd like your students to see. This is the introduction of yourself the students will see.

Course-Specific Setup for Instructor

As the online course instructor for PSYCH 112, there are several steps to take to prepare you for teaching the course. Before the course starts, you will want to register for a free account with Screencast. This site allows students to setup a free account and share their Jing screen recordings from this site. You will want to record a few practice Jing recordings. You will also want to have an account set up for LinkedIn and if possible explore BYU-I Connect.

  • Jing: Allows students to record their screen (or powerpoint) and then upload their recordings to Screencast.com.
  • Campbell Skills and Interests Survey: This survey costs $12 and is available online from the Academic Discovery Center.
  • Kaplin Self-proctored GRE Exam: Students will need to take a practice Graduate Record Examination http://www.kaptest.com/gre/gre-practice/free-gre-practice-test
  • BYU-I Connect: BYU-I Connect is a career networking site available here: http://www.byuiconnect.com/s/1085/07-idaho/idaho-start.aspx?gid=4&pgid=61 The site is the university’s alumni connections  network and puts students in contact with BYU-Idaho employees and other people throughout the United States who have volunteered to answer students' questions about specific careers.
  • LinkedIn: Students will need to register for a LinkedIn account in order to complete part of an assignment.
