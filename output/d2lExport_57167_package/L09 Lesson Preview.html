Learn

  • Learn, Do, Become Wheel (1 min)
  • Announcements - Check your instructors announcements for the week.
  • Questions and Conversations - For your questions about the course or your assignments.
  • Supplemental Reading -Prophetic teachings relevant to this week's scripture block.
  • Digging Deeper - Watch at least six videos that provide insight into the doctrines and principles of this week's scripture block.

Do

  • Directed Study - Select an option from the Directed Study Guide to help you focus on a significant principle or theme from this week's scripture block. 
  • Share it! - Share a scriptural insight with your friends, or family.

Become

  • Christlike Attribute Project - For this project you will select one Christlike attribute, search the Book of Mormon for teachings on that attribute, make specific goals to work on that attribute for at least two weeks, and then submit a 3-4 page paper detailing what you learned about the attribute from the scriptures and from your efforts to improve on that attribute in your life.
  • Personal Study Report - Report on your completion of this week's activities.
